Usage

Shuckle is a very great tanky setup pokemon with the highest combined defense in the game. In order for it to gain the best defense stats it has the worst health, speed, and attack stats. When shuckle is paired with a leech seed set up he can be a monster alone. The stalliest 1v6 sweeper... shuckle. Throw a binding band on shuckle and he can use contrary shell smash to stay tanky. Multiplying those 230 base defensive stats is too strong in numbers he can ernd up with over 2 thousand defense and special defense. Infestation having a 48PP and 100% accuracy for a trap move granting %health damage instead of scaling dmg. Toxic is also optional if you dont want to stress over roost or dont want binding band. binding band allows you to equip a sticky web set up incase shuckle falls you can always use a revenge or setup mon to clean up after shuckle's massacure. If only more people used this set shuckle would seriously be UU at minimum instead of all tiers. Finally once you feel like you are low you always have a choice to rest incase you are statused and/or low.
