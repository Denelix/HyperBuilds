Usage

Gastrodon is a great counter for choice water users. You can simply switch to gastrodon as kyogre and dracovish do not have great grass moves or really any at all. Gastrodon haigh HP and decently high Sp.D for it's health stops many special walls. Incase of a switch to something stronger vs what people believe is weak. High chances they will switch to a physical pokemon easy for you you can just Acid armor or if you have scald just scald and they should do way lower damage to you. Using a special attacking set for icebeam or earthpower you can counter your opponent with your storm drain ability and deal high amounts of damage than they ever espected. Gastrodon is sometimes used in higher elo over quagsire becuase of his base HP and stormdrain counter attack.
